Beat together 200 grams of sugar, 200 grams of unsalted butter, 4 large eggs, 200 grams flour, 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon baking powder, half a teaspoon vanilla extract, 2 tablespoons milk and a pinch of salt

Divide the mixture between your tins.

Bake for 20 minutes. To check if the cake is cooked, insert a skewer into the centre of the cake. If the skewer comes out clean, that means the cake is cooked

Leave the cake to cool in the tin for 10 minutes

If you want buttercream, use your store bought buttercream and decorate your cake with buttercream
